Who am I?

A charismatic, self motivated, Technical Writer seeking an opportunity to contribute expertise to a dynamic team. Also a short fiction author under pen name Sarkahn A.M

I enjoy writing in my free time as well as reading, learning new scientific developments or tech trends, developing new stories or world development and programming. You are always free to ask about my passion projects - I have a few.

It all started with tutoring my course subjects to classmates and friends that needed help in English and the natural sciences, which turned into writing about solutions on platforms like Chegg and the like. My writing took off when I knew I could break down most concepts into written form for others to digest, regardless of subject matter. I further involved myself in the scientific world which lead to my profession as a laboratory analyst and then later into a software engineer in test developing automated test cases and quality control test plans.
